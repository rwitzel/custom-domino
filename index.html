Design your own dominoes

You need

  • some photographs*
  • a printer
  • scissors
  • glue
  • some card board

Five steps to your tiles

  1. Set number of symbols
  2. Upload images
  3. Set print options
  4. Print
  5. Glue and Cut

Enjoy.

1 Choose the number of different symbols

2 Upload images *

Click on a tile to replace it with your own image


3Set print options

4 Print


5 Glue and cut

This step is handwork and it will take some time. Good luck!

We would be glad if you post your result on Facebook.

  • * Your images are not transferred to the internet. They are kept locally in the browser. Even if you edit them within the page.
